About Kieran Strawmier

My Name is Kieran Strawmier. I am a sophomore at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln double majoring in sports media and mass communication & advertising and public relations. My career interests are in the advertising/public relation field. I am looking to build experience with these skills in the sports industry. As a result of the course, (SPMC350), that required me to make this website, I am now looking to improve on my sports data analysis to potential pursue a career in that industry. In my personal life I am involved with UNL’s peer mentor program, I am currently a lead peer mentor helping to further develop the material for the course and being a mentor to ~8 freshman in the college of journalism. In my leisure time I enjoy sports in general and I am a member of UNL’s poker club.
